# Acceptable Use Procedure

**Companion to:** Acceptable Use Policy
**Document Owner:** CISO
**Version:** 1.0

> 📖 **Instructions:** Write step-by-step procedures for the key operational aspects of your Acceptable Use Policy. The Information Security Procedure (worked example) shows the format. Replace `> **✏️ YOUR WORK**
> YOUR WORK` sections with numbered steps someone could follow.

---

## 1. New Employee Acceptable Use Acknowledgment

> **✏️ YOUR WORK**
> YOUR WORK: Write the steps for getting new employees to acknowledge the Acceptable Use Policy.
>
> Think about:
> - When in the onboarding process does this happen? (Day 1? First week?)
> - Who presents the policy? (HR? IT? Direct manager?)
> - How does the employee acknowledge it? (Signature? Digital form?)
> - Where is the acknowledgment stored?
>
> Write 4-6 numbered steps.

---

## 2. Software Installation Request Procedure

> **✏️ YOUR WORK**
> YOUR WORK: Write the steps for employees requesting new software.
>
> Think about:
> - Who submits the request? (The employee)
> - Who approves it? (IT? Manager? Both?)
> - How is the software vetted for security? (Is it on an approved list? Does IT review it?)
> - How is it installed? (IT does it? Employee self-service from approved catalog?)
> - How is it tracked? (Software inventory)
>
> Write 5-7 numbered steps.

---

## 3. Acceptable Use Violation Reporting

> **✏️ YOUR WORK**
> YOUR WORK: Write the steps for reporting and handling a suspected policy violation.
>
> Think about:
> - How does someone report a violation? (Email to IT? Incident reporting tool? Manager?)
> - Who investigates? (IT? Security? HR?)
> - How is the investigation documented?
> - What are the escalation thresholds? (Minor → warning, Major → formal process)
> - How is the outcome communicated to the reporter (if appropriate)?
>
> Write 5-8 numbered steps.

---

## 4. Annual Policy Review and Re-Acknowledgment

> **✏️ YOUR WORK**
> YOUR WORK: Write the steps for the annual cycle of reviewing and re-acknowledging the policy.
>
> Think about:
> - When does this happen? (Same time each year? During security awareness training?)
> - How are employees notified?
> - What's the deadline for re-acknowledgment?
> - What happens if someone doesn't complete it?
>
> Write 4-6 numbered steps.
