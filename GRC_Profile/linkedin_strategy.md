## 4-Week Content Calendar

Post 2-3 times per week for 4 weeks to build momentum. Here's a framework — write the actual post drafts below.

### Week 1: Announce Your Journey

| Day | Post Type | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Text post | Announce your GRC learning journey and what you're building |
| Wed | Insight | Share one thing you learned about security policy writing |
| Fri | Quick tip | Share the policy vs. procedure distinction (it's always a hit) |

### Week 2: Risk Management Insights

| Day | Post Type | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Insight | Explain the 5x5 risk matrix (visual if possible) |
| Wed | Lesson | Share a non-obvious insight about risk treatment strategies |
| Fri | Engagement | Ask your network: "What's the most overlooked risk in startups?" |

### Week 3: Compliance & Frameworks

| Day | Post Type | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Insight | Explain why cross-framework mapping saves companies time |
| Wed | Value post | Share 3 things you'd do first if dropped into a company with no compliance program |
| Fri | Portfolio | Share your GitHub repo with a walkthrough of the compliance matrix |

### Week 4: Technical + Career

| Day | Post Type | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Story | Share your experience learning Python for GRC automation |
| Wed | Blog share | Share your blog post about the learning journey |
| Fri | Call to action | "I'm looking for GRC Analyst/Engineer roles — here's what I bring" |
