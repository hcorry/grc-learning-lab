# Resume Bullets

> 📖 **Instructions:** Transform your project accomplishments into impact-driven resume lines. Good resume bullets follow the pattern: **[Action verb] + [What you did] + [Scope/Scale] + [Result/Impact]**
>
> A worked example is provided for each category. Write 2-3 more bullets per category based on YOUR actual project work.

---

> Developed and documented 5 information security policies (Information Security, Acceptable Use, Access Control, Incident Response, Data Classification) with companion operational procedures for a 100-employee cybersecurity startup

## Tips for Using These Bullets

1. **Customize per application** — Pick the 3-5 strongest bullets that match the specific job posting
2. **Add metrics where possible** — "15+ risks" is better than "multiple risks"
3. **Lead with the most relevant category** — GRC Analyst role → lead with Governance or Risk. GRC Engineer → lead with Technical.
4. **Don't list everything** — A resume is a highlight reel, not a comprehensive list
